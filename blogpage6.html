Game of Thrones

Review by Kartik Arora

Book Review

We all have good and evil in us. There are very few pure paragons and very few orcs. A villain is a hero of the other side.

Game of Thrones is a fantasy novel series by George RR Martin where nine noble families fight for iron thrones to gain control of the mystical lands of Westeros while an ancient common enemy returns after being dormant for thousands of years. A thriller series that sparks every nerve of the body and creates the world of the mystic powerplay, leaving one to picture portray them as the crown jewel of the dynasty, who can command its empire to light and day.

The novel inspires one to dream big and work hard to achieve it. It helps a person to understand that chaos is a ladder. Many worthy to climb it fail too and never get to try again. The fall breaks them apart. And some are given a chance, but they refuse. They cling to the realm, god, or love which are all illusions. And like this in our lives, if we just sit around, thinking things can happen later or someone else can do it and we will get the fruit of their work. No, this can never happen. We have to realize that life is full of possibilities and we should take charge of life, embrace it because there is a lot that can happen between now and never.
